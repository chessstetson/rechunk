=======================

----------------------
Botox   : spreads the size of a dime
Dysport : spreads the size of a nickel
Daxxify : spreads the size of the Daxxify cap

CLINICAL GUIDANCE
-----------------
These ranges are intended as general dosing guidelines. Individual treatment
should always be tailored based on clinical assessment.

Consider:
• Are the muscles symmetric?
• Where does the patient fall on the strength spectrum?
• Do they prefer to preserve some dynamic movement or seek maximum stillness?
• Gender differences
• Facial anatomy
• Aesthetic goals

The art of treatment lies in recognizing that precision comes from understanding
nuance — not just numbers.


STANDARD RECONSTITUTION GUIDELINES
----------------------------------
Botox®   : 2 : 1
Dysport® : 1 : 2
Daxxify® : 1 : 1


CASE SCENARIO
-------------
Patient: Derrick
Age: 35
Reason for visit: Wrinkle relaxer consultation

Background:
• Strong frontalis and glabellar muscles
• Previous Botox treatment with minimal results
• Values longevity and minimal movement
• Wants a polished masculine look
• Describes desired skin appearance as “like a glazed donut”
• Interested in treating forehead lines and glabellar furrow


TREATMENT GOAL
--------------
Smooth, structured appearance without feminizing or arching brows.


CLINICAL CONSIDERATIONS
-----------------------
• Male patients often require higher dosing due to greater muscle mass.
• Frontalis must be balanced to avoid heavy brow or over‑arched effect.
• Desire for stillness + past under-response suggests higher-end dosing.
• Longevity, precision, and expression control are ideal indications for Daxxify.
